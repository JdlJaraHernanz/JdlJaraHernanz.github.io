Studies

This bachelor is taught completely in English which is not common in this level of studies in Spain. Consequently, I have made extensive use of my first foreign language throughout the courses.

As well, this bachelor has touched on several different branches of social sciences like political science, economics and law. Mainly these subjects have been studied in their broad global approach, for instance, with comparative political science or international law courses. Still my studies have provided me a multidisciplinar lens when looking at different social, political and economic phenomena.
