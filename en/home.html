Personal statement

The world is now facing an unprecendented decay of institutions at all levels around the globe. The principles of economic of liberalism are now at peril due to the vanity and self-righteousness of a few while the democratic institutions that were believed to be "everlasting" start to shake before our very own eyes. Many either give up or are reluctant to listen to the warning signs that threat all of our livelihoods and our standard of living. Yet, I still that there is hope even in these bleak times when many believe that the right thing to do is just but to look for themselves.


I hope that with my work, no matter where I develop myself professionally, I can properly give hope and improve peoples' lives in a substantial way, with different proyects that address key socio-economic problems. That my work can really channel peoples' proyects to new heights. As well, I want to be a citizen civically engaged with the European proyect since it is the one that can actually construct bridges between different peoples, which is the first step towards more international integration globally. Because once we understand what we share instead of what divides us, we can drive human ingenuity further than ever before.
